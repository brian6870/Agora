Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, TSC number, ID number, email, school, county
  • KYC Documents: ID card images, face photos for identity verification
  • Device Information: Device fingerprint, IP address, browser type
  • Voting Data: Anonymized vote records for audit

How We Use Your Information

  • Verify identity for voting eligibility
  • Ensure one person, one vote through device binding
  • Process and record votes securely
  • Communicate important election information
  • Maintain audit trails for election integrity

Data Protection

Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using AES-256 encryption. We implement strict access controls and regular security audits.

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections
  • Request deletion (subject to election record retention)
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

Data Retention

Election records are retained as required by union regulations. After the retention period, personal data is securely deleted or anonymized.
