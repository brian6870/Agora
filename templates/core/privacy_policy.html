At Agora, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, TSC number, ID number, email, phone number, school, county
  • KYC Documents: ID card images, face photos for verification
  • Device Information: Device fingerprint, IP address, browser type
  • Voting Data: Vote records (anonymized for audit)

How We Use Your Information

  • Verify your identity for voting eligibility
  • Ensure one person, one vote through device binding
  • Process and record votes securely
  • Communicate important election information
  • Maintain audit trails for election integrity

Data Protection

Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We implement strict access controls and regular security audits. KYC documents are stored securely and access is limited to authorized personnel only.

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections to your data
  • Request data deletion (subject to election record retention requirements)
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

For privacy-related inquiries, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@agora.ke
