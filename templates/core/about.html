Agora is a secure, civic-focused voting web application designed specifically for teacher unions in Kenya. Our platform ensures transparent, verifiable, and accessible voting mechanisms for union leadership elections.

Named after the ancient Greek gathering place for democratic discourse, Agora brings modern technology to union democracy, ensuring every voice is heard and every vote counts.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted digital voting platform for democratic organizations across Africa.

Our Values

Transparency, Security, Inclusivity, and Integrity in every aspect of our platform.
