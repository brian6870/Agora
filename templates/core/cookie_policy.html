Agora uses cookies to enhance your experience and maintain security. This policy explains how we use cookies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites remember your preferences and authenticate your sessions.

How We Use Cookies

Essential Cookies

Required for platform operation. Used for session management, security, and maintaining your login state.

Preference Cookies

Remember your theme preference (dark/light mode) and other settings.

Security Cookies

Help prevent fraud by supporting device fingerprinting and rate limiting.

Cookie Duration

  • Session cookies: Expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain for up to 30 days or until cleared
  • Device fingerprint: Stored securely for account binding

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality, especially device binding and login persistence.

By continuing to use Agora, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.
